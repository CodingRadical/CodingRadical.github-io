Hello my name is Safi Gharwal. I am a student at the NPower Canada Organization that is hosted by Toront's Ryerson University. The NPower Technology Service Corps (TSC) program gives aspirant IT professionals such as myself the technical knowledge and practical application experience needed to be job ready in the IT industry. I am currently enrolled in the Development Stream of the NPower program.

The program has allowed me to obtain my Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA 98-375) certification in HTML5 Application Development Fundamentals. This is an industry recognized certification that gives individuals a foundational knowledge of web development and how the three core front-end languages; HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript work together to make web pages and web applications. Personally, this certification has given me entry level proficiency in the three front-end web development languages and a working knowledge of Application lifecycle management and Agile development. This portfolio page – among other great projects that I have worked on in the duration of this program – is a great attestation to the development knowledge that I have acquired from the MTA certification.

The NPower program will further introduce us to Java programing and Android Mobile Development and will give us the opportunity to utilized our knowledge in real work place environments through an internship. For these and many other important reasons, this NPower program has been an amazing opportunity for me to enhance my web development knowledge and skills that I have learned through self study for over a year now. I am very confident of graduating this program by the end of November 2016. After the graduation I will pursue my goal of becoming a Master JavaScript Engineer through continuous learning and practical experience in web development applications by seeking employment in the IT industry.
